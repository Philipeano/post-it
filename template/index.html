Who can join?

Everyone! Regardless of your age, location, social status or gender, you're welcome to sign up and use PostIT!

Join... Connect... Share!


PostIT is a simple app for small or large group conversations.

It's the perfect platform for both formal and informal chats.


Sign up takes less than 2 minutes, and it's completely free!

Click here to get started.

How does it work?

  1. Sign up as a user, if you're new here.
  2. Create one or more user groups as you wish.
  3. Add other registered users as members of your group.
  4. Post messages in the group and view messages from others.
